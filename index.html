McNovak

William Alan McNeice  ·  Katarzyna Nowak-McNeice

Films about confinement, and the power that grows inside a closed room.

McNovak is the working partnership of William Alan McNeice, out of theatre, and Katarzyna Nowak-McNeice, out of literature. We make chamber pieces: a few people, one apartment, a door that will not open. The interest is always the same — what a person becomes when the world narrows to a single room, and who they decide to rule inside it.

The work is deliberately austere. It is carried by actors and by dread rather than by spectacle, closer to the quiet menace of Ring or Dark Water than to anything that needs a bigger budget to frighten you. Restraint is the method, not the apology.

We may be confined to protect us from the outside, or we may be confined to protect the outside from us. From Claustrophilia
